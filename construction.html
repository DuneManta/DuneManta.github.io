Building a mobile structure

The rules for constructing a mobile structure are deep and complex. With multiple different motive types, structure strengths, and restrictions on their size and equipment they're allowed to mount based on their chosen attributes. As an example, only reinforced structure styles are allowed to mount large military grade weapons. Anything using a lighter construction class is limited to using much smaller and less capable weapons. Similarly the airship motive type can't use higher classes of construction strength at all, and have a lot of forced restrictions as a result. The higher your construction strength is, the harder to damage and destroy your final structure will be. It also affects your maximum allowed size, with the stronger construction types being allowed more hexes for equipment and other items.
